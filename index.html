Another Place

A curated list of delicious and unique dining options beyond the typical campus canteens. From Telyu Coffee to the Food Court in front of campus, and Tel-U Grill, there's something for every palate. Reserve your spot today and experience the local flavors of Telkom University.

Tel-U Coffee

Telyu Coffee is a cozy and inviting cafe located within Telkom University that offers a warm and welcoming atmosphere for students and faculty alike. The cafe serves a variety of delicious coffee blends and specialty drinks, as well as a selection of tasty snacks and pastries. Whether you're looking for a quick caffeine fix or a comfortable spot to study or relax, Telyu Coffee is the perfect destination.

The food court in front of Telkom University is a bustling hub of activity where students and faculty gather to enjoy a diverse selection of delicious meals and snacks. With a wide range of food stalls offering everything from traditional Indonesian dishes to international cuisine, there is something for everyone to enjoy

From fresh meats and seafood to a variety of vegetables and sides, there's no shortage of options to choose from. So grab your apron and fire up the grill for a one-of-a-kind dining experience at Telyu Grill.
